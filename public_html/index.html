We are Early Years Playgroup

Welcome to Early Years Playgroup and Pre-School, we’re a charitable organisation managed by a voluntary parent committee. We are dedicated to educating and caring for children aged 2 to 5 years of age. We try to be flexible around you by offering morning, afternoon or full day sessions.

About Us

We have been providing quality care for children since 1986. In all that time we have only grown stronger in our understanding of what really matters to parents, when it comes to child care. Through the utilisation of our many childcare policies, an effective key person system, and a focus on working in partnership with parents at every step, we aim to ensure that your child receives a start that is second to none.

We strive to develop a child’s interests by providing a varied and interactive curriculum. We believe that learning through play is the best way to begin your child’s education. It underpins all aspects of your child’s development, their language, emotional, social and intellectual skills. We utilise both indoor and outdoor play as this allows children to explore new environments. They can put their ideas into practice, showing imagination and taking on new challenges either working alone or with others, in a safe and secure surrounding.

Holiday Club

We run ‘Holiday Club’ during certain days throughout the school holidays. We run a range of exciting activities for children aged 2-6, including crafting, baking, woodwork and dance workshops! During Holiday Club our sessions run the same.

Our sessions are:

9am to 12pm

12pm to 3pm

or the full day is 9am to 3pm.

Costs are £10 for the 3 hour sessions and £20 for the 6 hour sessions.

Meet the team

We are a dedicated and highly qualified team who support your child’s learning journey, through a range of stimulating and fun activities. We have secured close links with the local schools in the area, and work hard to support every child’s transition into school. We also believe it is important to provide support to every parent facing their child’s transition into school and are always happy to chat and offer advice.

We provide you with regular updates on your child’s progress and encourage all parents and carers to take an active role in supporting their child here at Early Years. To find out more about how you can get involved please contact us either through our chat box, on our Facebook page or by coming in to see us!
